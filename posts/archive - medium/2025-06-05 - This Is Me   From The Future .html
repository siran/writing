This is me, from the future. I still remember the whole journey, from the writing of this article, to before, and after.

It was not until today, June 5, 2025, that I for the first time, truly feel a Thursday like if it were a Sunday. I do not feel the urge to do something anymore, which led me to write "How to do nothing" (https://medium.com/@anrodz/how-to-do-nothing-a2a2b406ba62).

I do not have much time free either. Instant after instant I find myself following my passions (which could include joyfully just being.) It is very exiting, indeed.

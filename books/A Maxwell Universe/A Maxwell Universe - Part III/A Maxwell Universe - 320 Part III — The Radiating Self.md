## The Physics of Will (Telekinesis)

More radically, this implies that the "Will" has a direct mechanical coupling to external matter.

In Part II, we showed that "Force" is just the pressure of a field on a knot.
If the mind generates a field, and that field exerts pressure, then **Mind can apply Force.**

* **Subtle against what?**

An object on a table is in equilibrium. The forces upon it are balanced. In a system at balance, *any* new force is significant. The displacement is not negligible; it is simply opposed by friction and gravity.

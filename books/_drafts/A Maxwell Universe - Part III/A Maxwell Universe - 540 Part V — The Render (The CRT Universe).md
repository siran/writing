know why all electrons have the same charge and the same mass... Because they
are all the same electron."*

Wheeler suggested that what we see as a trillion particles is actually **one
single particle** threading back and forth through spacetime. In the Maxwell
Universe, we refine this. There is no "back and forth in time." There is only
the **High-Speed Present**.

## The Refresh Rate (Causal Speed)

Why do we see solid objects? For the same reason we see a picture on an old
television. The Cathode Ray Tube (CRT) shoots a single electron beam. It scans
the screen so fast that the human eye (which integrates over $\sim 50$ms) sees a
solid image.

**Matter is a Persistence of Vision artifact.** The "Solid World" is a
long-exposure photograph of a single topological defect moving at $c$.

This explains the speed limit of the universe. Why can't you travel faster than
light? Because $c$ is the **Scan Speed** of the Stylus. You cannot render an
object faster than the beam can draw it. When a Revolution happens—when the
Hyper-Construct shifts—the Reality does not change "instantly" ($t=0$). It
changes at **Causal Speed**. The Stylus must physically redraw the new shadow,
pixel by pixel, propagating out at $c$.
